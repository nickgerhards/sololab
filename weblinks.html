My favorite websites

YouTube

A media viewing platform most well known for videos. It can also be used for live streaming, live TV and sports, and Shorts, which is very similar to TikTok.

Hoopgrids

This is a daily puzzle game that I've been a huge fan of over the past couple of years. It stemmed from the idea of Wordle, but the objective is to guess a player that corresponds with both criteria on the X and Y axis. At the same time, you want to get as low as a score as possible, which means how often other players guessed the same player.

W3Schools

An educational website that has helped me with all things coding. Not only is it just used to get down proper syntax, but I've also used it a bunch to understand how certain concepts work and how they differ from syntax that is similar.
